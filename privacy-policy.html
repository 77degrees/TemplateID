Privacy Policy

Extension: Legal Hold Template Extractor – ST Legal

Last updated: 2025-11-08

Overview

This extension inspects fetch and XMLHttpRequest JSON responses in the active page to surface Legal Hold Template IDs and associated template names in a local, floating panel. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Collection

  • No data is collected, stored on external servers, or transmitted off the device.
  • The extension does not use analytics, tracking, or remote logging.

Data Usage

  • Detected values are kept in-memory for the current session and may be exported by you via a CSV download initiated from the panel.

Permissions

  • <all_urls> (or user-scoped “site access”): required to observe JSON responses on pages where you choose to use the extension.

User Controls

  • Toggle the panel with Ctrl + Alt + L.
  • Use the Clear button to remove the in-memory list.

Contact

For questions or issues, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
